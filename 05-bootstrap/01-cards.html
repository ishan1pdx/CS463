Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in Chrome.

Players control a dinosaur that jumps over obstacles and avoids enemies. It is simple, fun, and addictive.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019 by House House. It is a puzzle game where players control a mischievous goose.

Players complete tasks, annoy villagers, and cause chaos in a quiet village.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013 by Dong Nguyen. It became famous because of its simple design and difficulty.

Players tap to keep the bird flying between pipes without crashing.
